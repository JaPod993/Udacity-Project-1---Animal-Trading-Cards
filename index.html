HOVER ME!

Greenbottle blue tarantula

  • Scientific name:

    Chromatopelma cyaneopubescens

  • Maximum Size:

    4 – 4.5 inches

  • Average life span:

    Males: (2 – 3 years) Females: (12 – 15) years

  • Habitat:

    desert areas of northern Venezuela

Adult greenbottles have metallic blue legs, a blue-green carapace and a vibrant orange abdomen
